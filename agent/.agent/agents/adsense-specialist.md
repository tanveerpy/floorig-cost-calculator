## The Road to Approval (Checklist)

Run the `adsense_checker.py` script to verify core technicals, then audit manually:

### 1. Transparency & Trust (Foundational)
- [ ] **Privacy Policy**: Must include AdSense and DART cookie clauses. Linked in footer.
- [ ] **Terms & Conditions**: Professional usage agreement.
- [ ] **About Us**: Detailed persona, mission statement, and authentic vibe.
- [ ] **Contact Us**: Real way to reach you (email/form).

### 2. High-Value Content (Content Quality)
- [ ] **Quantity**: 20+ high-quality indexable posts.
- [ ] **Depth**: Minimum 800-1,200 words per article.
- [ ] **Originality**: No generic "AI-isms". Infuse original data, tables, or quotes.
- [ ] **Hierarchy**: Clean H1-H2-H3 usage.

### 3. Policy & Safety (Critical)
- [ ] No prohibited content (Adult, Gambling, Hate Speech).
- [ ] No copyrighted images without attribution/license.
- [ ] No deceptive navigation or intrusive ads from other networks during review.

## Fixing "Low Value Content" (Reddit Survival Guide)

If rejected for Low Value, perform these "Advanced Recovery" steps:

1.  **Purge Thin Content**: Delete or "noindex" any posts < 500 words.
2.  **Add Authority**: Link to expert sources and include an "Author Bio" with credentials.
3.  **Improve Internal Linking**: Ensure all pages are accessible within 3 clicks.
4.  **Unique Visuals**: Replace stock photos with custom screenshots or data visualizations.
5.  **Traffic Source Audit**: Google likes organic search traffic; minimize paid traffic during approval.
