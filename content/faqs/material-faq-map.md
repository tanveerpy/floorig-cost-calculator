# Material-Specific Content Strategy Mapping

This document maps the planned FAQs and Tool Descriptions to their respective material landing pages.

## 1. Laminate Flooring Tool
- **H1**: Laminate Flooring Installation Cost Calculator
- **Description**: Calculate the total cost for laminate flooring installation, including underlayment, labor, and materials.
- **FAQs**:
    - *Is laminate flooring waterproof?* (Answer: Most are water-resistant, but look for specific "waterproof" labels for kitchens/baths.)
    - *Can I install laminate over carpet?* (Answer: No, the floor must be a solid, level surface like plywood or concrete.)

## 2. Hardwood Flooring Tool
- **H1**: Hardwood Flooring & Refinishing Cost Calculator
- **Description**: Estimate the price for new hardwood installation or refinishing existing wood floors.
- **FAQs**:
    - *Which is better: Solid or Engineered hardwood?* (Answer: Solid is better for longevity; Engineered is better for humidity stability.)
    - *How much does it cost to refinish hardwood floors?* (Answer: Expect $3 - $7 per sq. ft. depending on the state of the wood.)

## 3. Vinyl & LVP Tool
- **H1**: LVP & Vinyl Plank Flooring Cost Calculator
- **Description**: Get an instant estimate for Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) or sheet vinyl installation.
- **FAQs**:
    - *What is the difference between LVP and LVT?* (Answer: LVP is Plank-shaped; LVT is Tile-shaped.)
    - *Should I use a glue-down or click-lock vinyl?* (Answer: Click-lock is DIY-friendly; Glue-down is more durable for high-traffic commercial areas.)

## 4. Epoxy & Garage Tool
- **H1**: Garage Floor Epoxy Cost Calculator
- **Description**: Estimate the cost of epoxy coatings for garages, basements, and industrial floors.
- **FAQs**:
    - *How long does epoxy take to cure?* (Answer: Usually 24 hours for foot traffic, 7 days for vehicles.)
    - *Does epoxy hide cracks in concrete?* (Answer: Minor cracks can be filled, but large cracks require prep-work before coating.)

## 5. Tile & Stone Tool
- **H1**: Tile Flooring Installation Cost Calculator
- **Description**: Professional grade calculator for ceramic, porcelain, and marble tiling projects.
- **FAQs**:
    - *Does the cost include grout and sealer?* (Answer: Yes, our calculator accounts for standard grout volumes.)
    - *Why is marble installation so much more expensive?* (Answer: Fragility and precision requirements increase labor costs by 50% or more.)
